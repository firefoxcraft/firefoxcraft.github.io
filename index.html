We offer fresh and honest cuisine featuring local products and Unique flavors. A young, passionate and creative team always working with pleasure with fresh food and a multitude of quality spices to concoct simple dishes, showing an aromatic depth and a concern for a job well done. The minimalist and eclectic decor composed of several recycled, raw and reworked elements invites usability and familiarity. A project created from scratch to share a passion for cooking, the pleasure of the table and the importance of taking this time to stop around a meal with loved ones.

Lunch menu at the slate, changing every week depending on the season and local arrivals. Possibility of "take-out" for lunch and dinner, served in ecological containers.
